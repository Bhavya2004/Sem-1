Profile

Prof. Maulik Trivedi

Assistant Professor


M.Tech. (CSE)

He is associated with the Darshan Institute of Engineering and Technology, Rajkot since July-2009. His areas of interest are Wireless Network, Computer Networks, Cyber Security and Google Services. He has worked as department placement coordinator from 2013 to 2018. Currently, he is coordinating alumni relations matters in the CE department. He is a life member of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE)..
